Hangman Directions!
1-If you have selected two player mode, hit the two player button
2-If playing one player, go about regularly and click the know category button to know the category
3-If you still can't get the word, you are allowed to use the hint button
4-HAVE FUN!(P.S: When you play two player, some buttons will disable, expect some to be not clickable!)
